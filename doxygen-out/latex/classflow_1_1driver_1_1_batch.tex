Captures the N oldest data elements. 

\begin{DoxyVerb}   Establishes a sequencing range with where <code>range.lower_stamp</code> is the stamp of
   the oldest captured element, and <code>range.upper_stamp</code> is the stamp of the newest.
   Removes oldest captured element from buffer.
